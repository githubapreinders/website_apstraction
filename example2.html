Tafeltrainer

Tafeltrainer is an educational web application where children can practise the times tables. At the same time Teachers and Parents are being held informed about the pupils progress. The app can be visited at

www.ikbenslimmer.nl

The app strives to motivate children to practise by a system with virtual money and goods they can buy in a little shop. Money can be earned by practising.

In the shop all kinds of products can be bought like animals, funny pictures, football players and so on. But when all the money is spent the child has to go to practise again!

The child has access to a personal wallet and a results page.

The app distinguishes two types of motivation: money and achievement. The latter category can earn prizes, while the money motivated pupils will go for the items in the shop.

For parents and teachers detailed insights are available by visiting the administration console. Since teachers face large time constraints they can be supplied with result lists by email.
