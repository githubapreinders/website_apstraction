Tafeltrainer

Tafeltrainer is an educational web application where children can practise the times tables. At the same time Teachers and Parents are being held informed about the pupils progress. The app strives to motivate children to practise by a system with virtual money and goods they can buy in a little shop. Money can be earned by practising. Since teachers face large time constraints they will be supplied with result lists by email. Detailed insights are available by visiting the administration console. The app can be visited as www.ikbenslimmer.nl

Gouda Walks

Gouda Walks is an app for Tourists to easily walk an interesting route while getting some interesting information. The app uses the GoogleMaps V2 Api and has separate designs for small and larger devices. Users can easily find their own location, toggle between map and information, zoom in and out and choose between map displays. The design is aimed at simplicity while at the same time being robust and taking in account the need to be thrifty with battery power. Built in languages English, Dutch, Spanish and French. Available from Api 10 onwards.

Groupmaker

Groupmaker is an application for teachers to easily split their groups in subgroups. In a relatively simple interface the teacher can divide his pupils into pairs, triples or whatever distribution is necessary, and display it on the electronic whiteboard if necessary. The app makes it possible to deviate from purely random distributions by making it the teacher possible to veto certain pupil combinations or likewise to make sure certain people to be in the same group. Also the app can divide certain properties evenly across the divisions. Results can be shared with collegues via sms, mail or via a connected bluetooth device. Applicable for Android devices from Gingerbread (Api 10) and upwards.
